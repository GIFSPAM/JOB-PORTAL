================================================================================
                    🔎 ADVANCED SORTING & FILTERING LOGIC
================================================================================
These parameters can be applied to Public Job Searches and Employer Applicant lists.

[ PARAMS FOR JOBS ]
- sort_by=salary      : ASC/DESC (Find highest paying jobs)
- sort_by=date        : Newest/Oldest (Find fresh postings)
- filter=location     : Match specific city (e.g., Palakkad)
- filter=skill_id     : Match specific technical requirement

[ PARAMS FOR APPLICANTS ]
- sort_by=experience  : Most experienced candidates first
- filter=proficiency  : Filter for 'advanced' skill levels
- filter=skill_id     : Filter candidates by a specific tech stack
